APPLICATION PROCEDURE

I. Make the specified payment at any bank nationwide on Remita platform and obtain Remita payment Receipt
II. Click on "Confirm Application Fee" and supply the RRR Payment Code on your Remita payment Receipt
III. If Confirmation of Application Fee is successful, you will be instructed to login with your RRR Payment Code, if not pls contact the helpdesk and log your complaints
IV. After successful login, please fill the form accordingly, upload your recent passport photograph and click on the submit
V. Print the acknowlegment and await sms for your admission information on phone soon.
VI. MAKE SURE YOU LOGOUT FROM YOUR APPLICATION WHEN YOU ARE DONE.

STUDENT REGISTRATION PROCEDURE======[PLEASE READ CAREFULLY FEW THINGS HAVE CHANGED AROUND HERE]


FOR FRESH AND RETURNING STUDENT

Step 1 [How do i login?]

  • Login with your JAMB Registration/Application No.[Fresh Students Only] OR Matric Number as Username and your Surname (in CAPITAL OR UPPERCASE) as Password
  • You will be required to supply your valid Email Address and Phone Number and change your password before you can proceed
  • In case you forgot your password, click on the forgot password link "Get it Here" and supply your JAMB Registration OR Matric Number, your Registered Email address and Phone Number and your new password and click on send to reset your password and send to your registered Phone Number and Email Address

Step 2 [Student Biodata]

  • Click on Student Biodata
      • For Fresh Students and Returning Students
    • It is compulsory that you fill the biodata sections accordingly. Under the Upload section, you will be required to upload the scanned copy of your Passport(not more than 1 month old)
    • click on "Finalize and Preview Biodata Registration" under the Finalize section
    • After clicking on Finalize, print your Biodata Report and GOTO BURSARY DEPARTMENT TO GENERATE YOUR INVOICE(REMITA RRR OR ETRANZACT TRANSACTION ID)
    • Please Note: You will not be able to generate school invoice and make payment at the bank if you fail to complete these sections appropriately.
    • Please also note that for returning students, it is also compulsory for you to review your biodata and submit but you may not print your biodata report.

Step 3 [Payment: FOR BURSARY STAFF AND STUDENT]

  • Click on Payment
  • Your payment due will be available as Debit under Student Ledger
  • To make any payment, you have to select the payment type, generate invoice from which your RRR NUMBER for FULL-TIME STUDENTS OR TRANSACTION ID for PART-TIME STUDENTS will be made available
  • Present the RRR NUMBER OR TRANSACTION ID on the Invoice at any of the Banks listed on your invoice and make the payment on REMITA PLATFORM OR ETRANZACT PLATFORM as the case may be
  • You have to confirm your payment under the Confirm Payment Section and Print your Official Receipt

Step 4 [Course Registration]

  • Click on Course Registration
  • To be eligible for course registration, you must have paid and confirmed your SCHOOL FEE
  • Your courses will be displayed for you select appropriately and submit , print and present to the appropriate Registration Office for approval
  • After approval, your must print another copies, present them for endorsement and submit at your department.

Any further enquiries and complaints should be reported to our LiveChat System Below OR sent to [email protected], we will be there to assist you.
